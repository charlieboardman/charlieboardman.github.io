Rolling Budget

Settings

Daily increment

Each new calendar day adds this amount. Missed days are caught up the next time you open the app.

Set my balance to

Cheat mode. This adds a visible adjustment to the ledger; it does not erase your history.

Complete log

Download every increment, transaction, and balance adjustment as a CSV file.

Backup

Your data exists only in this browser. Save a backup regularly. Restoring replaces the current budget.

Install this app

iPhone or iPad: open in Safari, tap Share, then “Add to Home Screen.”

Android: open the browser menu and tap “Install app” or “Add to Home screen.”

Stored only on this device and browser. No account, server, or synchronization.
